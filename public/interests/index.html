Comics/Manga/Webtoons

I haven't read too many western comics that aren't webcomics. My current favourite webcomics are probably Paranatural and The Boy Who Fell. I think I prefer webtoons for how accessible they are, I'm subscribed to a couple on the app.

As for manga, I also don't read them a lot unless I hear of one that particularly vibes with me, or I really need to know what happens after the end of an anime when I know it's not gonna get another season. My current favourite might be Witch Hat Atelier. When it comes to reading manga and webcomics, I'll read all of what's out all in one go, forget about it for a few months, then come back and catch up on what's left. So assume I'm not up to date on anything!!
